    \textbf{Towers of Hanoi}
    
    \begin{lstlisting}
    def towers(n, a, b, c):
        if n == 1:
            print("Move disk from %s to %s"%(a,b))
        else:
            towers(n-1, a, c, b)
            towers(1, a, b, c)
            towers(n-1, c, b, a)
    \end{lstlisting}
    
    Recurrence equation:
    
    Time(1): 1 move
    
    Time(n): Time$(n-1)$ + Time(1) + Time$(n-1)$
    
    Closed form: $O(2^n) - 1$
